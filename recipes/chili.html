Chili

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 beer
  • whole clove of garlic
  • tabsco chili starter
  • spicy V8 juice
  • spicy chili beans
  • great northern beans
  • dehydrated peppers
  • tablespoon of bacon grease (substitute with butter)

Steps

  1. roast dehydrated peppers in pan on medium heat until skin starts to blacken.
  2. place roasted peppers in pot of boiling water until soft
  3. peel and roughly chop onion and garlic
  4. using the bacon grease, fry the chopped garlic and onions in a pot on medium heat until the onions begin to brown
  5. chop rehydreated peppers and add to pot
  6. add ground beef to pot and mash until fine
  7. stir pot until ground beef starts to turn brown
  8. add the rest of your ingredients to pot
  9. cook on medium high heat for one hour, stirring continuously
  10. lower heat to medium low and cook for four to six hours, stirring ocasionally (the longer you cook it the better it tastes)
  11. serve
