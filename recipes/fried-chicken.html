Fried Chicken

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of chicken
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 3 eggs
  • table spoon of chili powder, ginger, white pepper, paprika, and salt
  • 2 cups of oil (preferrably peanut)

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl large enough to fit any of your chicken pieces
  2. Heat the oil in a deep pan until 350 degree fahrenheit
  3. Mix the flour with the chili powder, ginger, white pepper, paprika, and salt
  4. Cover the chicken in the flour mixture
  5. Dip the chicken in the beaten eggs until covered in the eggwash
  6. Cover the chicken in the flour mixture again
  7. Place chicken carefully in the hot oil, laying the pieces away from you as you put them in
  8. Cook until golden brown (should take between 7 and 10 minutes)
  9. Repeat until all the chicken is cooked
