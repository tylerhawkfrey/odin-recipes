Burger

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of hamburger
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Quarter cup of Parmesan cheese
  • Quarter cup of Italian dressing
  • Tablespoon of salt and pepper
  • Eight strips of bacon
  • Barbecue Sauce
  • Mayo
  • Four slices of smoke gouda (you can use any cheese, smoked gouda is my favorite)
  • Four buns

Steps

  1. Mix the burger, parmesan cheese, italian dressing, salt, and pepper, kneading with your hands. Divide into four even sized balls.
  2. Fry bacon in a pan on medium heat to prefered crispiness. Remove from pan but leave hot grease.
  3. Flatten one of the balls of burger into a disk.
  4. Fry in bacon grease, two minutes on one side and one minute on the other.
  5. Clean tomato. Peel onion. Slice both into prefered thickness.
  6. Spread barbecue sauce on one side of the bun and mayo on the other.
  7. Stack all ingredients inside bun in any order.
  8. My oreferred order is onion, burger, cheese, bacon, lettuce, tomato, with barbecue sauce on the bottom bun and mayo on the top.
